Tails the worker log at <dataDir>/worker.log (default ~/.local/state/junco/worker.log, rotated at 10 MB). -f follows, -n N bounds the tail. Output is human-readable on a TTY and raw JSON when piped or with --json; --human forces the readable format even when piped.

junco logs -f
junco logs -n 200 --json
